## How to Upgrade

Follow these steps to ensure your documentation platform receives the latest updates.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Backup Your Site" icon="database">
    Export your current documentation content to avoid data loss.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Dependencies" icon="package">
    <CodeGroup tabs="npm,yarn">
````bash
npm update john-chen-docs@latest
````
````bash
yarn upgrade john-chen-docs@latest
````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache" icon="refresh-cw">
    Run `npm run clear-cache` or equivalent for your setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Changes" icon="check-circle">
    Verify feng shui course pages and enrollment forms work correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
