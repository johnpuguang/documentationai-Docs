## Add Your First Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Login and open your documentation dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Find and click the <b>New Project</b> button in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out details">
    Enter your project name and description, then save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    Begin adding pages, guides, or references as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
