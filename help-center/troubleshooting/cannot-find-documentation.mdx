## Symptoms

- Can't locate project pages
- Search returns no results

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Ensure you are logged in with the correct account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the search bar">
    Try keywords or partial matches for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask the owner">
    Reach out to John Chen or your admin to check if the docs exist or are private.
  </Step>
</Steps>
